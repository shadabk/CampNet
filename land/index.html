A network of students, for students, by students...

CampNet is your Campus Network. A social network just for your college. It is a place to meet new people, get to know about various events in college and share knowledge with your buddies.

All event notifications in one place. CampCalen is an update of what's happening in your campus

  • :: CampStore ::

    Get All your Needed Books At a Single Place

    CampStore is an online portal for students to buy or sell books. The seller remains anonymous to the users and only ads are visible. In the current stage, a user is limited to posting 10 ads. Simple user interface and efficient filters make the service a breeze to use. CampStore enables easy exchange of study material from seniors to juniors.
